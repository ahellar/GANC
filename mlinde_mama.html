About Mlinde Mama

The Mlinde Mama Digital Health Platform is an innovative maternal healthcare solution designed to improve pregnancy outcomes through artificial intelligence, digital health, and community engagement. It leverages advanced machine learning techniques to predict and stratify maternal risks early in pregnancy, enabling timely interventions and referrals. The initiative aligns with Tanzaniaâ€™s Ministry of Health recommendations through the Unified Community System (UCS), facilitating integrated, high-quality antenatal care.

Key Features

  • AI-Powered Risk Stratification: Advanced machine learning (XGBoost) with 90% accuracy.
  • Digital Health Integration: Implementation of electronic medical records (EMR).
  • Community-Driven Antenatal Care: Group Antenatal Care (GANC) fostering peer support.
  • Data-Driven Decisions: Analysis of over 300,000 ANC visit records.

Our Impact

  • Improved data quality and decision-making capabilities.
  • Increased ANC services attendance (ANC4+).
  • Better utilization of ANC interventions: Iron & Folic Acid, IPTp for Malaria, deworming, HIV & malaria testing, Haemoglobin checking.
  • Enhanced early detection of hypertensive disorders of pregnancy.

Our Team and Expertise
